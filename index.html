About Us !

M/S Shri Shaanthini Products has been wholesaling sanitary napkins since 2016 and is reckoned a notable player in the field of women hygiene . The noble plan of M/SSP is to penetrate sanitary napkins market with a sole aim of offering a practical solution to women hygiene. Maquitesafe sanitary napkins from M/S SSP are customized hygienic napkins and are safe to use by women of all age group. The jewel of our products is anion napkin which is very safe and comfortable for ladies' use. It empowers women with comfort and relaxation during menstrual time. Bamboo napkins and Herbal napkins are the latest addition to our inventory basket. Maquitesafe napkins are anti fungal ,anti bacterial ,chemical free with leak free protection and above all ecofriendly. Our pencil pack napkins ( Biscuit type) are suitable for sanitary napkin vending machines.
